Interest in IT

What is your interest in IT?

My interest in IT comes from a facination with Sci-Fi. Keeping up to date and using things like mobile phones, is the type of thing I could only dream of as a boy growing up, watching Star-Trek (TNG).

My intrest in IT started from a vrey young age when my father brought home a Commodore 386. This was the first time I'd ever seen a computer close up, and I just wanted to know how it works. I can't explain it with words deeper than that :)

My experience with IT is quite extensive. I studied about them my entire life, and work currently in the "Tech Industry". My jobs have been from lowly IT help desk support, to the Princpal IT consultant for the APAC region of a multi-national SaaS company.
